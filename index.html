About the event

We're a group of nomadic Jesus people traveling Aotearoa, and we love doing life together with the folks we meet. And everyone we encounter is searching. For hope, for meaning, for belonging — everyone desires those things. In this multi-day workshop we'll dive into what it means to love God, each other, and the world, and how the person of Jesus helps us do that. We'd love to have you with us.

Speaker

Mark Johnson

Mark hails from Minneapolis in the USA, where he helped run a Jesus Kitchen at the Rainbow Gathering for many years. He's journeyed alongside punks, artists, musicians, and hippies for decades, and even accidentally planted a church along the way. He and his wife Kirsty and their two boys live in Newtown, Wellington and have been part of the St Tom’s community since 2015.
